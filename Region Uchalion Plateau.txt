Overview
Uchalion Plateau is a misty Battanian highland realm of craggy mountains, ancient lakes, and winding rivers, serving as a strategic crossroads between northern bays and southern basins. Dominated by clans like fen Marunath, it blends druidic rites with Fian warfare, defending against eastern wilds and imperial incursions. False moons thin the veil, stirring prophetic whispers and rock knocks in the fog-shrouded terrain. Economies thrive on grain, herbs, furs, and wine from fertile meadows and wooded valleys, with settlements fortified amid standing stones and glacial streams—a land where ancient echoes meet Unveiling's subtle horrors.

Marunath (Town)
Sprawling timber longhouses cluster around sacred groves and standing stones atop misty craggy peaks, where clan councils convene under starlight amid alpine meadows and elusive mountain goats. Markets trade furs, amber, and herbs, harp songs echoing through narrow ravines with glacial streams. Druids perform rites at ancient oaks on windswept ridges, while Fian archers train in sheer-faced clearings. False Moon nights cause unnatural rock knocks, prophetic dreams haunting the garrison, countered by ritual fires and blessed herbs.
