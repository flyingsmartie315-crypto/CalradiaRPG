Weapon Cultures of Calradia
Full Cultural Naming System + Cosmetic Tag System
Version: 2026-03-19

All mechanical values remain identical across every culture and every weapon.

=== How the Tag System Works ===
- Append 1–4 tags in brackets after the base weapon name.
- Tags 1–3 = Cultural / Aesthetic flavour
- Tag 4 = Condition / Durability (always last)

=== Condition / Durability Tags ===
[Pristine] – Mirror-bright, freshly oiled, looks brand new
[Worn] – Honest patina, light scratches, well-maintained
[Battle-Scarred] – Proud nicks, repaired edges, honourable battle damage
[Damaged] – Bent, chipped, cracked grip
[Ruined] – Heavily rusted, cracked blade, blood-caked
