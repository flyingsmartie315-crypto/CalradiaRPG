Unveiling Commodity/Bounty Economy
Since the Unveiling, hunters, vermin-slayers, and coastal raiders harvest monstrous beasts for alchemists, armorers, and physicians. Guilds in border towns buy raw carcasses cheap, process them, and sell refined materials at premium to Pravend, Jaculan, and Charas buyers.

Prices in Calradia gc/sc/bc per typical harvestable unit from one adult specimen (fresh quality, border-region market). Values reflect rarity, utility, demand, and Unveiling-era scarcity.

Metallurgy of the Unveiling (Valyrian Steel)
When the False Moon swells low and heavy, comets blaze across the sky. From these falls come raw lumps of veil-fallen steel ore—cold, unearthly fragments called veil drop.
Raw veil drop (standard 200 g lump): 20 gc (worth its weight in gold).
Refined Valyrian steel (1 kg ingot): requires 1.6 kg raw veil drop.
Finished 2 kg ingot: 800–1200 gc in Pravend and Charas markets.
Only Thamaskene crucible forges can work it. Fortune seekers risk their lives during False Moon nights to claim it.
Trade & Black Market Dynamics
Primary hubs: border towns, coastal wrecks, ruined crypts, steppe caravans.
Risks: many items are heretical or treasonous. False Moon nights increase potency and danger.
