Order of the Silvered Star
A small, highly ascetic monastic order dedicated to guarding the sacred Pegasus pastures in the high alpine meadows of Vlandia. They are quiet sentinels against Unveiling corruption, especially in the thin-veil regions where the False Moon’s influence is strongest.
Core Tenets & Vows

Strict vow of poverty (members own nothing beyond what they can carry).
Vow of humility and service.
Vow to protect the sacred Pegasus herds at all costs.
Vow to combat sophisticated Unveiling threats (intelligent curses, thinking veil-beasts, possessed individuals, veil-rifts that require ritual combat).

Structure
The Order is deliberately tiny. There are only 14 full Templar Brothers at any one time. Most live in remote high-meadow hermitages, tending the Pegasus herds and maintaining ancient silvered relics.
The Silvered Archivists (Non-Templar Brothers)
In addition to the Templar Brothers, the Order includes a body of non-Templar Scholar-Brothers known as the Silvered Archivists. These men have taken up the sacred burden of cataloguing, studying, and understanding every manner of Unveiling menace.
The Archivists classify all Unveiling threats into two broad categories:
Malevolus et Invasivus (Malevolent and Invasive).
Most entries in the common bestiaries fall under Invasivus — physical creatures and beasts that invade the mortal world.
Malevolus, however, is where the true horror resides: ghosts, possessions, Waelhreow, and all other Malevolus entities are vile spawns and manifestations of the Infandum Malum — the unspeakable evil that first tore through the veil during the cataclysmic False Moon of 777 BE. The Archivists have spent centuries building what is arguably the most comprehensive understanding of the supernatural in Calradia, meticulously recording every report sent back by their field agents.
The Exorcista
The active crusading arm of the Order and the eyes of the Silvered Archivists. These are the field operatives who leave the alpine cloisters to hunt the worst Unveiling abominations across Calradia. They travel light, usually on foot with a pack mule, maintaining the vow of poverty. A Pegasus only manifests when the threat is truly beyond mortal means — a divine sign from the gods.
Notable Members
Brother Gregoris the Blessed — Veteran Exorcista
Age: ~150 years (appears mid-40s)
Height: 6'10"
A “blessed” mutant born under a violent False Moon. The mutations granted him superhuman strength, speed, agility, and dramatically slowed aging — officially recognised by the Order as divine favour.
He walks the roads of Calradia more often than not, leading a pack mule laden with seemingly mundane items. The only real valuables are basic provisions. All truly valuable equipment is strapped to his body and hidden beneath a long, plain monk’s habit to help him escape notice.
When trouble finds him he more often than not resolves it without drawing a blade, using instead his simple-looking but extremely valuable staff carved from the ivory of a leviathan.
A Pegasus only appears when the situation is truly dire. The contrast of this grim, blade-covered giant riding a majestic white Pegasus is both awe-inspiring and unintentionally comical.
Current Status
Since its inception the Order has amassed one of the most comprehensive understandings of the supernatural in all of Calradia. Their archives — particularly the Malevolus records — are said to contain knowledge that even the Imperial libraries in Charas would envy. The Silvered Archivists and their Exorcista eyes continue their silent vigil, quietly adding to humanity’s fragile defence against the ever-evolving horrors of the Unveiling.
