Luminescent Wisps (Eastern Steppes)
Floating orbs of soft blue light that appear on moonless nights over the vast steppes, guiding lost nomads or leading them astray. Born from Unveiling echoes of Khuzait sky beliefs, they pulse with faint whispers of forgotten prophecies. Harmless but enigmatic; some touch them for visions of fate, others fear madness. Rare, they drift near ancient burial mounds, vanishing at dawn.
