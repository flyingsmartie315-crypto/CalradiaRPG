Processed Goods (Derived from Primary Village Commodities)
Refined/manufactured towns/forges/mills/workshops. Values per standard stack/cart load(~100–200 units/equiv processed volume). Prices gc/sc/bc; denar equiv brackets gameplay ref. Fluctuate region quality supply(±50%).
Beer — Fermented grain(barley/malt); common drink preservation. 1 stack(barrels ~100L) → 1 sc [60 denars]. Wt:180kg Vol:1.2m³. Grain.

Wine — Fermented grapes; luxury drink trade staple. 1 stack(barrels ~100L) → 1 sc [60 denars]. Wt:180kg Vol:1.1m³. Grapes.
Steel — Refined wrought; balanced blades/armor. 1 stack(ingots ~100kg) → 5 sc [300 denars]. Wt:110kg Vol:0.5m³. Wrought Iron.
Fine Steel — High-purity; superior weapons/armor base. 1 stack(ingots ~100kg) → 8 sc [480 denars]. Wt:110kg Vol:0.5m³. Steel.
Whale Oil — Rendered fat; lamps soap leather treatment. 1 stack(barrels ~100L) → 2 sc [120 denars]. Wt:170kg Vol:1m³. Walrus Tusk(coastal whale hunts).
Leather — Tanned hides; armor boots saddles. 1 stack(sheets ~100 hides) → 1 sc [60 denars]. Wt:120kg Vol:1.5m³. Hides.
Planks — Sawn timber; construction ships furniture. 1 stack(boards ~100m² coverage) → 80 bc [400 denars]. Wt:300kg Vol:2.5m³. Timber.

Pottery — Fired clay vessels/tiles; containers roofing. 1 stack(vessels/tiles) → 30 bc [150 denars]. Wt:120kg Vol:1.2m³. Clay.

Tools — Forged iron implements(hoes axes hammers). 1 stack(20–30 tools) → 1 sc [60 denars]. Wt:100kg Vol:1m³. Iron Ore→Crude Iron→Wrought Iron.
Linen — Woven flax cloth; clothing sails bandages. 1 stack(bolts ~100m²) → 1 sc [60 denars]. Wt:90kg Vol:2m³. Flax.
Velvet — Luxurious woven fabric; noble clothing/trade. 1 stack(bolts ~50m²) → 5 sc [300 denars]. Wt:80kg Vol:2m³. Wool+dyes+advanced weaving.
Felt — Compressed wool; hats padding insulation. 1 stack(sheets ~100m²) → 50 bc [250 denars]. Wt:100kg Vol:2m³. Wool.
These processed goods represent next tier. Villages supply raw primary inputs; towns mills forges transform higher-value(typically 2–5× raw price). Barter rural common; coin urban elite trade. All advanced goods(armor weapons luxury) derive from these basics.
