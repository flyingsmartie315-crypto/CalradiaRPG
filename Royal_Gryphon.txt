Royal Gryphons
Royal Gryphons, majestic winged variants of the gryphon species, emerged with the Unveiling as ultra-rare apex predators in Calradia's mountains. Towering at 3.6 meters, they fuse an eagle's keen beak and eyes with a lion's powerful tawny body and vast feathered wings, enabling effortless soaring over vast distances. Always male and long-lived—spanning centuries—they hatch from standard gryphon eggs at a 1/1000 chance, emerging larger and airborne. Only a handful have been recorded, rarer than Pegasus, each forging legendary tales: Vlandia's "Stormwing" carried a Gryphon Knight to triumph against Sturgians; Aserai's "Skyreaver" shielded prophets from storms; Khuzait's "Windreaper" guided khans through blizzards. Their roars shake valleys, claws rend armour like paper.
They are non-aggressive unless provoked, preferring lone hunts or territorial patrols. When challenged, they dive with devastating force, talons extended, beaks snapping. Royal Gryphons bond only with the worthiest—those who prove courage and respect through perilous climbs and trials. A bonded rider gains a companion of unmatched loyalty, capable of scouting from clouds or turning battles with aerial strikes.
They share whimsy with standard gryphons — idly knocking stones or relics off precipices — but their scale allows pushing boulders or wagons, sending them cascading below with thunderous impact. Eggs, veiled treasures, lure knights to perilous ascents, where gales and talons claim the unworthy. To claim a royal is to court eternity's edge; in Calradia's veiled realms, they are luminous harbingers—beautiful, lethal, and whimsically destructive sovereigns of the skies.
