Direwolves
Giant wolves native to the North, direwolves adhere strictly to natural law—simply larger, smarter versions of ordinary wolves, not supernatural abominations. Unveiling amplified their size and cunning, but they remain beasts of instinct: pack hunters with iron-gray fur, eyes like glowing coals, howls carrying miles to coordinate ambushes. Fiercely protective, they defend bonded humans against all threats, including Unveiling horrors. Pups are highly sought as lifelong companions—Sturgians have deep affinity, bonding them young through shared hunts and trust. Rare south of Sturgia or deep Battanian forests.
