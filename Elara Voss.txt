Elara Voss
Age: Early 20s
Parentage: Illegitimate daughter of Duke Servic dey Valant and a common huntress from the Uchalion foothills.
Background: Raised discreetly in the court of Rovalt as a handmaiden and courtier. Despite no public acknowledgment, Duke Servic quietly ensured she was given a secure place in the household and received a proper education in letters, etiquette, history, diplomacy, and basic martial skills. She grew up surrounded by the intrigues and grandeur of her father’s domain, always aware of her hidden bloodline but never openly acknowledged. At 16 she cut her hair, stole away from home, and spent three years working her way north as a stable hand, caravan guard, and false squire before infiltrating The Eyrie and bonding her gryphon.
Appearance
Athletic and lean with a clear warrior’s physique — toned arms, strong shoulders, narrow waist, and confident, grounded posture. Short, dark black hair with natural volume and messy, windswept strands that fall across her forehead and frame her face. Striking green-hazel eyes with a sharp, focused, and slightly defiant gaze. Fair skin with a light natural blush on the cheeks. She wears a high-collared, quilted maroon/red quilted vest with intricate gold embroidery and reinforced leather straps, black quilted sleeves, blue trousers, and sturdy brown leather boots. She carries a practical, well-worn longsword with a simple crossguard.
Gryphon Companion
A powerful common gryphon (not royal) with deep brown fur, darker shading on the limbs, and golden-amber eyes. Tumbles was a bit of a runt when Elara first bonded with him — clumsy and constantly tumbling over his oversized paws during early training. He experienced a massive growth spurt shortly after bonding with Elara, rapidly becoming a formidable war-gryphon.
Elara affectionately nicknamed him “Tumbles”. Among the Knights Griffon he is informally known as “Sir Tumbles, Bane of Gargoyles” — a nickname earned during their very first outing into the wider region. While exploring the rooftops of Ostican, Tumbles spotted a stone gargoyle perched high on a church spire. True to the feline whimsy common to all gryphons — the idle habit of knocking loose stones or relics off precipices for no reason other than the satisfying tumble — he suddenly bounded up the spire, enthusiastically swatted the inanimate gargoyle off the ledge with a mighty paw, and watched it shatter spectacularly in the square below. Elara could only stare in disbelief as her overprotective (and slightly destructive) gryphon puffed out his chest with immense pride, clearly convinced he had just eliminated a deadly threat to his new rider.
Personality
Fiercely independent, quietly noble, dry wit, unyielding will, and a noticeable impulsive streak that often leads her into trouble. She is driven by a mix of resentment, curiosity, and a deep desire to prove she belongs in the world her father rules.
Skills & Abilities
Living among the martial environment of the Knight Brothers, she has picked up practical combat skills despite Vlandian tradition. She is a keen and accurate shot with a powerful recurve bow of notable draw weight, which she can use effectively both from the saddle and on foot.
Current Role & Status
Because martial affairs in Vlandia are a strictly male domain, Elara was not formally granted the title of Knight Brother. However, her extraordinary feat of bonding a gryphon could not be ignored. The Order patched together an honorary title for her: Courtly Ambassador of the Knights Griffon.
She now serves as the Order’s official envoy, frequently treating with the other and minor ruling clans rather than the royal court in Galend (where Preceptor Batistin dey Major already serves as the primary liaison). She represents the Brotherhood in diplomatic matters, relays intelligence, and negotiates gryphon support for campaigns or joint operations. There are some reservations within the Order about this appointment due to her impulsive nature, but the combination of her court upbringing, the undeniable success of her bonding, and the unseen protective hand of Duke Servic have ensured her position. She remains the only woman to hold such a formal role within the Knights Griffon.
