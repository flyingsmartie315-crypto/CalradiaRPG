Armour Cultures of Calradia
Cultural & Cosmetic Tag System
Version: 2026-03-19 (Final)

All mechanical stats (AC, Coverage %, Encumbrance, Durability Score, upgrades, layering, etc.) remain identical across every culture.

=== How the Tag System Works ===
- Append 1–4 tags in brackets after the base model name.
- Tags 1–3 = Cultural / Aesthetic flavour
- Tag 4 = Condition / Durability (always last)

=== Condition / Durability Tags ===
[Pristine] – Mirror-bright, freshly oiled, looks brand new
[Worn] – Honest patina, light scratches, well-maintained
[Battle-Scarred] – Proud dents, repaired marks, honourable battle damage
[Damaged] – Bent plates, torn lining, visible repairs
[Ruined] – Cracked, hanging by threads, heavily rusted or blood-caked

=== Special Rules ===
- Masks: only one mask tag per culture
- Vlandia: uses visor tags instead of masks on full-visored helms
- Automatic Visor Rule: visor tags activate only when Full Visor upgrade is present

Visor Compatibility:
[Klapvisor] – Bascinet & Sallet
[Houndskull] – Bascinet only
[Pigface] – Bascinet only
[Bretache] – Bascinet only
[Bellows] – Bascinet & Sallet
[Pointed] – Sallet only
[Jawbone] – Sallet only
