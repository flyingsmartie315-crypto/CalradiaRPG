Core Resolution Mechanic (d100)
All actions are resolved with a d100 roll (1–100).

Success = Roll ≤ relevant Characteristic or Skill value
Failure = Roll > relevant Characteristic or Skill value

Degrees of Success / Failure
Every Characteristic has a Bonus = tens digit (round down).
Example: Strength 41 → Strength Bonus = 4

Each point you succeed by = +1 Degree of Success (DoS)
Each point you fail by = +1 Degree of Failure (DoF)
Bonuses determine damage, distance moved, time saved, effect severity, etc.
