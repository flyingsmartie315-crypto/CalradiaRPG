New Category: Throwing Weapons
This new category consolidates all thrown missile weapons. They are one-handed, quick to ready (1 AP), and use the existing Throwing quality (range 6/12 yards base, –10 BS at long range). Damage scales with weight and type; improvised items are highly variable.
Throwing Quality Reminder

Range: Short 6 yards / Long 12 yards
Reload: 1 AP (ready + throw)
Can be used in melee at 0.5 yards with –10 WS

Improvised Thrown Object Rules (Catch-All)
Use this for anything not listed (rocks, bottles, chairs, etc.). Damage is based on weight/size:
Tiny (pebble, coin): (0 / 0 / 1) — 4/8 yards, negligible damage.
Small (thrown rock, mug): (0 / 1 / 2) — 6/12 yards.
Medium (brick, helmet): (1 / 2 / 3) — 4/8 yards, –10 BS.
Large (barrel, shield): (2 / 2 / 4) — 3/6 yards only, –20 BS, requires two hands to ready.
Massive (boulder dropped from height): (3 / 3 / 6) — 0 yards (drop only), treat as falling damage (see Falling rules). Cannot be thrown by normal humans.

Weight-Based Modifiers
+1 Blunt per 20 Enc of object.
If dropped from height (e.g., rooftop): Use Falling Damage table instead of thrown profile.

Additional Notes
Javelin: Can be used in melee as a short spear (Reach 1.5 yards).
Throwing Axe: Impact gives two d10 roll (higher).
Throwing Dagger: +20 Concealment when hidden.
All benefit from Throwing Ammunition rules (lead weights or sharpened edges add +1 Pierce).
Hulking+: +1 to all damage profiles and +2 yards short range.
