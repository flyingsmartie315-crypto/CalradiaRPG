The {Player} is a young man of imposing stature, standing at 6'5" with a lean, muscular build honed by years of disciplined movement and survival. His skin is very pale, almost luminous in certain lights, contrasting sharply with his dark blond hair worn at medium length and left flowing yet neatly controlled. His eyes are light grey, cold and piercing, capable of silencing a room with a single glance. His features are handsome in a patrician style—high cheekbones, strong jaw, straight nose—but carry a sharp, intimidating edge that makes people hesitate to approach. A harsh scar runs from his right brow down the full length of his face, the remnant of an old wound from youth that nearly split his face in two; it has mostly healed cleanly over time and is now only faintly visible as a thin, pale line—noticeable mainly to perceptive or close observers, especially in certain lighting.
He carries himself with quiet authority—every motion deliberate, precise, and economical, never wasted. His nature is brooding, silent, and aloof; he speaks little unless necessary, and when he does, his voice is low, measured, and carries an undercurrent of steel. Seasoned warriors need only glance at him once to recognise the danger: the way he stands, the calm readiness in his posture, the faint tension in his shoulders that speaks of a man who has killed before and will do so again without hesitation.
