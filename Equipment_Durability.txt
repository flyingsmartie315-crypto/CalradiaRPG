New: Helmet-Specific Durability Rules

All helmets start with –5 base Durability Score compared to body armour (more joints, visor weak point).
Rigid Uniform helmets: +15 Durability Score (solid monolithic construction).
Rigid Partial helmets: +5 Durability Score (segmented construction).
The visor zone (final 5% coverage) has –20 Durability Score (always the weakest point). If no Visor/Full Visor/Ventail is fitted, this zone is 0 AC and degrades twice as fast.

How the 95% Rule Interacts with Durability

Any helmet at 95% base coverage has its visor zone as the only open component (always 0 AC unless upgraded).
This 5% zone has the –20 Durability Score penalty and degrades twice as fast from combat hits or EHGS events.
Adding a Visor, Full Visor, or Ventail removes the penalty and protects that zone.
