Description
A massive segmented horror with thick rust-brown chitin plating and dozens of powerful hooked legs. The head is a nightmare fusion of skull and insect — multiple black eyes, massive serrated mandibles, and two enormous crushing pincers capable of shearing a man in half. It emits a low rattling hiss that echoes through stone like grinding bones.
Behaviour & Danger
Ambush predators that lurk near underground water sources in the southernmost craggy mountains of Aserai territory. They burst from side tunnels with terrifying speed, using their pincers to crush and drag prey deeper into the darkness. Their venom is paralytic and extremely potent. The hardiest Aserai tribes sometimes deliberately lure smaller specimens into kill-zones with goat sacrifices, harvesting their chitin for experimental scale armour and venom sacs for potent poisons.
The mythos has spread far — travellers now think twice before running into the shade offered by any rocky overhang or cave mouth in the southern highlands.
